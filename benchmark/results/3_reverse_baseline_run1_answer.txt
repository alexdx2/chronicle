## Producers

| Class | File | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| `BattleResultProducer` | `arena-api/src/arena/battle-result.producer.ts` | Called by `ArenaService.tomAttacksJerry()` after each battle event is persisted to the DB |

**What triggers a publish:** `ArenaService.tomAttacksJerry()` (line 49) calls `battleResultProducer.publish(event)` after computing the battle outcome (`TOM_HITS`, `JERRY_DODGES`, or `TRAP_TRIGGERED`) and writing the `BattleEvent` record to Prisma/SQLite. The publish happens on every `POST /arena/attack`.

## Consumers

| Class | File | Handler method | Pattern binding |
|---|---|---|---|
| `BattleResultConsumer` | `spectators-api/src/spectators/battle-result.consumer.ts` | `handleBattleResult(event)` | `@EventPattern('battle-results')` |

`BattleResultConsumer` depends on `SpectatorService` (injected via constructor) and delegates to `spectatorService.recordBattle(event)`.

## Services Involved

| Service | Role |
|---|---|
| **arena-api** | Sole **producer** ‚Äî orchestrates battles, persists `BattleEvent` to its own Prisma DB, publishes to `battle-results` |
| **spectators-api** | Sole **consumer** ‚Äî receives events, stores them in-memory, exposes leaderboard and recent-battles endpoints |
| **tom-api** | Indirectly involved ‚Äî `ArenaService` fetches `GET /tom/status` before each battle to get Tom's speed/id/name |
| **jerry-api** | Indirectly involved ‚Äî `ArenaService` fetches `GET /jerry/status` and `GET /jerry/traps` before each battle |
