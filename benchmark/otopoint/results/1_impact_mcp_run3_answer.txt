## Confidence: 92/100

**Why not 100:**
- `api/src/services/order.service.ts` is 2,393 lines — inner method bodies not exhaustively read; edge-case callers in test helpers and dev controllers may exist.
- `@generated/order/` directory (208 files) was confirmed by path but not individually verified — standard Prisma/Paljs gen means every field rename regenerates all 208 files.
- `payment.service.ts` `fulfillPayment()` internals (order creation on Stripe webhook) were described by the service but the exact `prisma.order.create()` call site was not line-verified.
- Any queue processor for auto-disconnect (mentioned in `ORDER_AUTO_DISCONNECT_IMPLEMENTATION.md` in docs) was not found as a Bull processor — it may be implemented via Socket.io disconnect events rather than a queue; not confirmed.
